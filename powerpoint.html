Social Media Brand Creation Project

In this project, I created a brand called The Jays Nest. It is a blog centered around providing news and updates about the Toronto Blue Jays. This presentation explains the social media platforms chosen to promote the brand, and the data driven reasons behind those choices.

Social Media Brand Evaluation Project

In this project, I evaluated the performance of my brand from the above project. I looked at social media metrics generated over several months and determined which platforms were the most successful in reaching my target audience. I also proposed possible solutions for any problems that arose.
