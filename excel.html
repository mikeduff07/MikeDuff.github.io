Bicycle Store Sales Project

In this project, I looked at customer & sales data from a bicycle store. I looked at variables such as income, work commute distance and age to determine which type of customer had the highest chance of purchasing a bicycle. I also created a dashboard to visualize the results. Skills used: pivot tables, nested IF statements, slicers, charts & graphs. Vehicle Manufacturer Sales Data Project
